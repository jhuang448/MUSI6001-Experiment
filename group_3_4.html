Experiment - Group 10


    In the experiment, you are required to memorize and reproduce rhythm patterns (by any means which make some sound).

    There will be totally 4 pieces of rhythm to memorize. (And also a testing piece at the beginning.)

    The LENGTH of the rhythm grows from 2 measures to 8 measures. (2, 4, 6, 8)

    The first half of the rhythm is the SAME as the second half. (e.g. a 6-measure piece may have the structure like: A B C A B C)

    MOVE along to the rhythm while listening!

    Click to record, click to stop. You have only one chance. JUST GO AHEAD!
